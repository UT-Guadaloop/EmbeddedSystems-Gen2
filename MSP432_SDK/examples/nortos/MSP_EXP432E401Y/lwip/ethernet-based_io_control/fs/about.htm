About Texas Instruments


Texas Instruments (TI) is a global semiconductor design and manufacturing company. We focus on developing analog chips and embedded processors. We also produce TI DLP™ techonology and education technology products. In the microcontroller space, TI offers the broadest range of embedded control products, including SimpleLink™ wired and wireless MCUs, ultra-low-power MSP430™ MCUs, and high-performance C2000™ real-time controllers. Read more about us at www.ti.com.
